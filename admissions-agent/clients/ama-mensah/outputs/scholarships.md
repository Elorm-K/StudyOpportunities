## Honest funding assessment (no over-promising)
Full funding for a Ghanaian taught master's is **realistic but competitive** — it rests on winning a
portable award (Chevening or Commonwealth most likely given the profile). Strategy: apply to
**both** Chevening and Commonwealth (different timelines/agencies), target at least one Mastercard
partner university, and treat GREAT as a partial fallback. If none land, UK universities rarely fund
international taught master's directly, so deferring to the next cycle or a lower-cost route should be
discussed. All figures per-cycle — re-verify via `knowledge-base-update` before the client applies.
