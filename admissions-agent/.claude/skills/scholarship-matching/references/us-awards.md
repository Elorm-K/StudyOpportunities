# US awards & funding routes reference

Detail for US scholarships AND the structural funding routes that matter most for full funding.
Authoritative figures + dates live in `kb/scholarships/us.md`; this is the working catalog with the
**gates** spelled out for hard-gating.

> Gate quick-reference for hard-gating. Authoritative figures, the need-blind-for-internationals
> list, and the funded-PhD model live in `kb/scholarships/us.md` (seeded 2026-06-20) — verify each
> school on its own page before presenting (the need-blind list shifts).

| Award / route | Level | Value | Nationality gate | Field gate | Need/other gate |
|---------------|-------|-------|------------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Fulbright (Foreign Student)** | Master's / PhD + research | Stipend + health + airfare; tuition varies by country | Non-US citizen; **apply via home-country Commission/Embassy** | All (clinical patient-care excluded) | **Merit-based**; US-bachelor-equivalent; English (TOEFL ~79–80 / IELTS 6.5+) |
| **Merit/need aid (undergraduate)** | UG | Institution-specific; rare full for intl | No national award; **most schools need-aware** for intl; tiny elite group need-blind + full-need (MIT, Princeton, Yale, Amherst, Harvard — verify per school) | Any | School-specific |
| **Funded PhD assistantship (RA/TA/fellowship)** | PhD (+ STEM research master's) | **Full**: tuition waiver + stipend + health, ~5 yrs | **Open to internationals** (visa ~20 hrs/wk limit) | Field/department-specific (strongest in STEM) | **Awarded by department/PI**, not central office; admission ≈ funding decision |

## Notes

- For US PhDs, the **funded-assistantship route** is usually the realistic path to full funding —
  prioritize departments that fund admitted PhDs over named external scholarships.
- "Fully funded" at the school level ≠ funded for internationals — verify on the program page.
- Undergraduate full funding for internationals is rare; pair with `kb/us/need-blind.md` and the
  funding-aware university shortlist.
- Verify current cycle dates + amounts via `knowledge-base-update` before presenting.
