# US application deadlines

> **Deferred (background priority).** Leading client countries (Bangladesh, Ghana, Nigeria) are
> verified first; US deadlines are filled **on-demand by `knowledge-base-update` per client**. The
> windows below are **conventions to confirm**, not citeable figures — never present them without a
> live source URL + date from the program/Common App.

## Undergraduate (Common App) — CONVENTIONS, verify
- Early Decision (ED) / Early Action (EA): typically **~Nov 1**.
- Regular Decision (RD): typically **~Jan 1–15** for fall entry.
- CSS Profile / financial-aid forms: due alongside the admission deadline (internationals).

## Graduate (direct to program) — CONVENTIONS, verify
- Funded-program applications: typically **Dec–Jan** for fall entry.
- **April 15** — Council of Graduate Schools acceptance convention (deadline to accept a funded
  offer). Confirm at councilofgraduateschools.org.
