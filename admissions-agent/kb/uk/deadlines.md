# UK application deadlines

> Dates below are verified from UCAS + GOV.UK (2026-06-20). UG dates are firm; **PGT/PGR are mostly
> rolling and program-specific** — those rows are not yet verified (research run was cut off). Cite
> the source when presenting; re-check each cycle (threshold 90 days).

## Undergraduate (UCAS)

| Milestone | 2026 entry | 2027 entry |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Oxbridge + medicine/dentistry/veterinary | **15 Oct 2025** (18:00 UK) | **15 Oct 2026** (18:00 UK) |
| Equal-consideration deadline (all other courses) | **14 Jan 2026** (18:00 UK) | **13 Jan 2027** (18:00 UK) |
| Last date for applications with choices (then → Clearing) | **30 Jun 2026** | **30 Jun 2027** |
| Final date for applications | 24 Sep 2026 | **23 Sep 2027** |
| Clearing window | **2 Jul – 19 Oct 2026** | **2 Jul – 18 Oct 2027** |

Source: ucas.com (verified 2026-06-20). 2027-entry equal-consideration (13 Jan 2027) and Clearing
close (18 Oct 2027) were 2/3-vote confirmations — re-confirm closer to cycle.

## Postgraduate taught (PGT)
**Deferred (background) — filled on-demand by `knowledge-base-update` per course.** Mostly
**rolling**; competitive courses (and those needing a visa) fill early — apply as soon as the cycle
opens. Confirm the specific course/university window live before presenting.

## Postgraduate research (PGR)
**Deferred (background) — filled on-demand per advert/department.** Funded-studentship deadlines
cluster around UKRI cycles (often Dec–Feb for the following autumn). See `kb/sources/funded-positions.md`.

## UK Student visa (GOV.UK, verified 2026-06-20)

- **Earliest to apply:** 6 months before course start (applying from outside the UK); 3 months before
  (from inside the UK).
- **Processing time:** usually **3 weeks** (outside UK) / **8 weeks** (inside UK).
- Plan the visa step backwards from these in `timeline-planning`. Source: https://www.gov.uk/student-visa
