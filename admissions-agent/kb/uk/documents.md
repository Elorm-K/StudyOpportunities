# UK required documents by level

> **Deferred (background priority).** Conventional baseline for `document-checklist`; the exact list
> is confirmed **on-demand by `knowledge-base-update`** against the target university's admissions
> page per client. Leading countries (Bangladesh, Ghana, Nigeria) are prioritized for verification.

## Undergraduate (via UCAS)
- Personal statement
- Academic reference
- Academic transcript / predicted or achieved qualifications
- English language test (IELTS/TOEFL) if applicable
- Passport (for visa/identity)

## Postgraduate taught (PGT)
- CV/résumé
- Personal statement / statement of purpose
- Two academic (or academic+professional) references
- Transcript + degree certificate
- English language test
- Passport

## Postgraduate research (PGR)
- **Research proposal** (and often prior contact with a prospective supervisor)
- CV/résumé and writing sample/publications
- Two academic references
- Transcript + degree certificate(s)
- English language test
- Passport
