# UK scholarships

> `scholarship-matching` **hard-gates** on nationality, degree level, field, and funding need BEFORE
> ranking (see `.claude/skills/scholarship-matching/references/eligibility-gates.md`). Each £ figure
> below is a per-cycle rate the provider revises annually — tag the cycle and re-verify before
> presenting. Deadlines below are recurring annual windows; specific dates marked "not published"
> need a live check via `knowledge-base-update`.

| Award | Level | Value | Nationality gate | Field gate | Other hard gates | Source |
|-------|-------|-------|------------------|-----------|------------------|--------|
| **Chevening** | Taught **Master's only** (1-year) | **Full**: tuition + monthly stipend (London/non-London split — *amount not published officially*) + return airfare + arrival/departure allowances + visa cost | Citizen of a **Chevening-eligible country/territory** | All subjects (must fit career goals) | **2,800 hrs (~2 yrs) work experience** post-undergrad; apply to **3 eligible UK courses**, hold ≥1 unconditional offer by deadline; **must return home ≥2 years** after award | chevening.org |
| **Commonwealth Master's** | Taught/research Master's | **Full**: tuition + **£1,452/mo stipend** (**£1,781/mo London**) + return airfare + £225/yr thesis grant + visa | Citizen/refugee/BPP **resident** in an eligible **low/middle-income Commonwealth country** (44-country list) | All subjects, mapped to a CSC development theme / UN SDG | ≥ **2:1** honours (or 2:2 + relevant PG qual); **must be unable to afford** UK study without the award; **apply via a Nominating Agency** (no direct applications) | cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk |
| **Commonwealth PhD** | PhD (tenable **36 months**) | **Full**: tuition + **£1,452/mo** (**£1,781/mo London**) + airfare + visa | **Two streams** — (a) **least-developed / vulnerable states** (19-country list, incl. Nigeria, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Kenya); (b) **high-income** Commonwealth (different, lower stipend £1,378/£1,690 — do not conflate) | All subjects, dev-theme/SDG aligned | ≥ **2:1** (or 2:2 + PG qual); unable-to-afford; via Nominating Agency | cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk |
| **Gates Cambridge** | PhD, MPhil, MLitt, MRes, LLM at Cambridge | **Full-cost**: full tuition + maintenance **£22,050 / 12 mo** (2025-26 single rate; up to 4 yrs PhD) + airfare + visa + IHS; discretionary family/fieldwork/development funds | **Any country outside the UK** (US round = US citizen+resident) | Any subject at Cambridge | Must be applying for / admitted to an eligible full-time Cambridge postgraduate course; **no return-home requirement** | gatescambridge.org |
| **Clarendon (Oxford)** | All Master's + DPhil (PhD) at Oxford | **Full**: full course fees (Home & Overseas) + living grant ≥ UKRI min (**2025-26: ≥ £20,780**) for the fee-liability period | **All nationalities — no restriction** | No field restriction | **No separate application** (auto-considered on Oxford graduate application by the funding deadline); outstanding academic merit (most hold ≥ First); **no return-home requirement** | ox.ac.uk/clarendon |
| **GREAT Scholarships** | 1-year taught **Master's only** | **Partial**: **≥ £10,000** toward tuition (some universities more, e.g. £15,000) | Citizen of a **specific eligible country published per cycle** — **2026-27 (18):** Bangladesh, China, Egypt, France, Ghana, Greece, India, Indonesia, Italy, Kenya, Malaysia, Mexico, Nigeria, Pakistan, Spain, Thailand, Turkey, Vietnam | Varies by university/strand (General, Sci&Tech, Justice&Law, Climate) | Enrol on an eligible Master's at a participating university; **apply direct to the university** (no central deadline) | study-uk.britishcouncil.org |

## Deadline windows (recurring; verify exact dates per cycle)

- **Chevening** — opens ~early Aug, closes ~early-mid Oct (2025-26 closed 7 Oct 2025 12:00 UTC). 2026-27 dates not yet published.
- **Commonwealth (Master's & PhD)** — opens ~autumn; nominators forward to CSC ~December; outcomes ~July; study starts Sept/Oct. 2026/27 round closed.
- **Gates Cambridge** — opens ~early Sept; US Round ~mid-Oct, International Round course-specific (~early Dec / early Jan). Reopens Sept 2026 for 2027/8 entry.
- **Clarendon** — tied to Oxford's December/January graduate funding deadlines; divisions review Feb/Mar.
- **GREAT** — varies by university (spring/early-summer of entry year; 2026-27 examples: Manchester 23 Apr, Glasgow 30 Apr, Cardiff 31 May 2026).
