# UK scholarships

> `scholarship-matching` **hard-gates** on nationality, degree level, field, and funding need BEFORE
> ranking (see `.claude/skills/scholarship-matching/references/eligibility-gates.md`). Each £ figure
> below is a per-cycle rate the provider revises annually — tag the cycle and re-verify before
> presenting. Deadlines below are recurring annual windows; specific dates marked "not published"
> need a live check via `knowledge-base-update`.

## Deadline windows (recurring; verify exact dates per cycle)

- **Chevening** — opens ~early Aug, closes ~early-mid Oct (2025-26 closed 7 Oct 2025 12:00 UTC). 2026-27 dates not yet published.
- **Commonwealth (Master's & PhD)** — opens ~autumn; nominators forward to CSC ~December; outcomes ~July; study starts Sept/Oct. 2026/27 round closed.
- **Gates Cambridge** — opens ~early Sept; US Round ~mid-Oct, International Round course-specific (~early Dec / early Jan). Reopens Sept 2026 for 2027/8 entry.
- **Clarendon** — tied to Oxford's December/January graduate funding deadlines; divisions review Feb/Mar.
- **GREAT** — varies by university (spring/early-summer of entry year; 2026-27 examples: Manchester 23 Apr, Glasgow 30 Apr, Cardiff 31 May 2026).

### Exclusions & traps (do NOT offer these to NG/GH/BD clients)

- **Saïd Foundation** — funds only Syrian, Jordanian, Lebanese, Palestinian nationals. **Excludes all three.**
- **Charles Wallace Bangladesh Trust** — **CLOSED** (wound up 31 Aug 2025). Do not offer. (Charles Wallace trusts only ever covered India/Pakistan/Bangladesh/Burma — never Nigeria/Ghana.)
- **Manchester Equity & Merit** (six East/Southern African countries) and **Beit Trust** (Malawi/Zambia/Zimbabwe) — exclude all three target nationalities.
- **Rotary Foundation Global Grants** — a *route*, not a fixed award: requires a sponsoring Rotary club/district in both home and host areas + ≥US$30,000 budget + one of Rotary's 7 focus areas. Catalog as a route, not a guaranteed award.
- **GREAT per-university country trap:** GREAT eligibility is set per-university per-cycle. Among catalogued schools — Nigeria is *excluded* from GREAT at Edinburgh/Nottingham/Manchester; Ghana qualifies at Edinburgh/Sheffield/Manchester; Bangladesh at Sheffield/Manchester; only Warwick lists Nigeria (and excludes Ghana/BD). Always check the specific university's current GREAT list.
- **Naming trap:** Warwick **Chancellor's International Scholarship is PhD-only** despite the name (open to all overseas-fee nationalities for research clients).
