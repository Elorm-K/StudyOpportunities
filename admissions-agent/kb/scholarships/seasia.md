> Used by `scholarship-matching` for **nationality-based matching** — the nationality/origin gate is
> the KEY hard gate. **Advising note:** most government scholarships here carry a **return-service
> bond** or a return undertaking (must return home / cannot switch to a settlement visa). That is a
> real hard gate for a client whose goal is to settle abroad — surface it explicitly.

## Cross-cutting (also applies to this bucket)
- **Chevening** (UK, taught Master's) — open to citizens of Chevening-eligible countries incl.
  Vietnam, Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, Malaysia, Bangladesh. See `kb/scholarships/uk.md` for
  full terms (2,800 hrs work experience; 2-yr return-home requirement).
- **Fulbright Foreign Student** (US) — apply via the home-country Commission/Embassy; rules vary by
  country. See `kb/scholarships/us.md`. (Eligibility/terms for these specific countries **not
  verified this run**.)
- **Commonwealth PhD (LDC/vulnerable)** — **Bangladesh is listed first** among eligible countries.
  Full: tuition + £1,452/mo (£1,781 London) + airfare, 36 months. Gates: ≥2:1 (or 2:2+Master's),
  permanent residence, **financial need** ("unable to afford UK study"). ⚠️ **Return obligation:**
  signed undertaking to return home ASAP, **cannot switch to a UK graduate/settlement visa**, no CSC
  support to stay, must leave the UK within 1 month of award end — a hard gate for clients aiming to
  settle abroad. See `kb/scholarships/africa.md` for the full 19-country list.
- **GREAT Scholarships** — Bangladesh is on the 2026-27 eligible-country list (taught Master's,
  ≥£10,000). See `kb/scholarships/uk.md`.
